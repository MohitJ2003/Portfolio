🏋️ 6-Day Workout Plan – Amarpali Enchante Community Hall GYM

Training Tips:
  • Rest 60–90 sec between isolation sets; 2–3 min between compound lifts.
  • Progressive overload: increase weight or reps each week.
  • Warm up 5–10 min before each session (light cardio + dynamic stretches).
  • Stay hydrated throughout your session.
  • Day 7 = Full rest or light walk/stretching.
